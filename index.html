Bit Manipulation

An interactive tutorial from place values to floating point.

This tutorial teaches binary representation, bitwise operators, and common bit-manipulation patterns — from first principles to real-world applications including IEEE 754 floating-point formats used in machine learning.

Every concept has a hands-on demo. Toggle bits, adjust values, and watch the results update live. No prior knowledge of binary arithmetic or hardware is assumed.

Chapter 1 Binary Place Values How bits combine to represent any whole number. Chapter 2 Signed Integers Two's complement and the origin of negative numbers in binary. Chapter 3 Bitwise Operators AND, OR, XOR, NOT, and shifts applied bit-by-bit. Chapter 4 Bit Shifts Logical and arithmetic shifts as multiplication by powers of two. Chapter 5 Bit Tasks Get, set, clear, toggle, and update individual bits. Chapter 6 Masks Building and applying single-bit and range masks. Chapter 7 Packing and Unpacking Storing multiple values in one integer using bit fields. Chapter 8 Applications Permissions, log₂, and other real-world bit patterns. Chapter 9 Floating Point IEEE 754 from sign bit to NaN, covering fp32, fp16, and bf16.
